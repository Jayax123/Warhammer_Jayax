ADMIN COMMANDS

  • Home: Takes you to the home entry, where a brief introduction can be found.

  • Help: Lists all available commands for the user.

  • Overview: Provides a basic overview of the current situation on the planet. Type "Overview" for the most recent report or enter "Overview [number]" with a specific number to view a past overview.

  • Granvalis Prime: Introduction to Granvalis Prime.

  • Compound: Describes a unique pesticide used on Granvalis Prime.

  • Harvest Ledger: Report on where a year’s harvest is going.

  • Tavian Lirae: Background on the current leader of the Administratum on the planet’s surface.

  • VX-09K: Background on the current leader of the Mechanicus on the planet’s surface.

  • Fevil Mancar: Background on the current leader of the Ministorum on the planet’s surface.

  • Daran Maron: Background on the current commander of the Planetary Defence Forces on the planet’s surface.

  • Elira Campestris: Data gathered on the daughter of Marcus Campestris, the planetary governor.

  • Marcus Campestris: Background on the planetary governor of Granvalis Prime.

  • Nocth: Summary of Nocth presence and forces on Granvalis Prime.

  • Carn: Report on the leader of Nocth forces on Granvalis Prime.

  • Ventoria: Short summary of Venotria City, the largest city on Granvalis Prime.
