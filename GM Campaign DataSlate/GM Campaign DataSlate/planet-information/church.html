Lux Veritatis ("The Church")

Summary

The Lux Veritatis, or "The Church," is one of the few holy locations on Ganvalis Prime. Due to the planet's sparse population, it is difficult for churches and similar institutions to be built. As a result, this is the main center of the Ministorum's power. Most people on the planet's surface hold prayers and religious observances in their homes or residences rather than in large churches.
