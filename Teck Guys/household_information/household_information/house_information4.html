Renown Methods

  • Renown through Lineage: Through your
    lineage and the furthering of its
    prestige, your house gains great renown.
    • Single Bonus: Gain 100 Renown if a great monument, fortress, or city is built in your familyâ€™s name, serving as a symbol of its reign.
    • Multi Bonus: Gain 10 Renown whenever you personally achieve something significant that others recognize. This can be awarded three times per campaign.
  • Renown through Trade: Through the beneficial trade of goods and services, you gain renown for yourself and your household.
    • Single Bonus: Gain 100 Renown for making a lucrative deal with an allied force while in a campaign. This deal can be anything, but must be beneficial for your force in some way. This can be claimed once per campaign.
    • Multi Bonus: Gain 10 Renown if you sell a good you brought to a campaign with you. This can be claimed three times per campaign.
  • Renown through Hunting: Through fighting and defeating enemies larger than normal you gain renown for you and your household.
    • Single Bonus: Gain 100 Renown if you vanquish an army level solo threat such as astartes champions, a grand cruiser or battleship, or superheavy sized enemies. Can only be claimed once per campaign.
    • Multi Bonus: Gain 10 Renown for vanquishing solo enemies considered significant threats by you and your allies such as a small group of chaos space marines, a cruiser or battlecruiser, or a tank squadron. Can be claimed up to twice per battle.
