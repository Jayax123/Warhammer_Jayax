House History

House Nomenica has existed for a long time, originally forming on a mining planet that was lost to the Imperium for centuries. Over time, it evolved into a noble household that developed and controlled much of the trade in technology and weaponry. They thrived in this role for generations, but eventually, the Imperium rediscovered the planet. Upon recognizing the planet's wealth of valuable resources, the Mechanicus resumed mining operations there.
