Over time, House Nomenica has slowly
grown in power through illegal means,
operating primarily on the outskirts of
Imperial space areas where the Mechanicus
is less likely to find them. They strive to maintain a low profile, content to be perceived as a weak noble house trading in common goods. This low profile is critical, as the discovery of their illegal trade could spell the end of the entire house.

House Nomenica operates in smaller branch families that follow the commands of the main family; these members are the only people other than the main family within Nomenica that have power. These branch families are often highly specialized in a branch of technology which they develop, or in a particular aspect of combat which they provide to the main family. Nomenica soldiers are sourced largely from cloning though some are also gotten from the general populace both however are modified, brainwashed, and altered to serve only the house loyally.

Inheritance

  • Armory x 40 (840 Renown): House Nomenica is renowned for its rare equipment. The inheritance includes 10 pieces of light power armor, 5 graviton weapons, 5 Displacer fields, 5 volkite weapons, and 5 blast pistols.
