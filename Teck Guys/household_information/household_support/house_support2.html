House Venit (Military Household)

  • Level: 2
    • Level 1: 10 Force% worth of your army
      comes from this household, and behave
      at 1 Veterancy Level higher than what they should be.
    • Level 2: This 10 Force% also comes with a free Level 1 Fighter. Also receive a 5 Force% discount on a Level 2 military champion within your force who hails from this Household.
  • Champions: Zeniph
  • History: House Venit specializes in producing reliable troops and skilled commanders. As the second most powerful branch family, and the strongest to support Macille in any capacity they hold significant influence. A more religious house with unique beliefs, House Venit is known for its exceptionally well-trained and fiercely loyal forces. While their soldiers typically receive more modest augmentations compared to others, their discipline and leadership set them apart.
