House Trium (Support Household)

  • Level: 2
    • Level 1: One support-focused advisor
      receives a 5 Force% discount for
      Level 2. Support-focused advisors include techpriests with support disciplines, arbitrators, judges, remembrancers, sages, navigators, clerks, and medicaes. The first Mercenary purchased is half their Throne cost.
    • Level 2: Two support-focused advisors now receive a 5 Force% discount for Level 2. The second Mercenary purchased is also half their Throne cost.
  • Champions: Vecal
  • History: House Trium has long been known for its advanced technological development, specializing in the design and innovation of cutting-edge technology. While they excel at creating new and useful inventions, their production capacity is limited. They possess vast resources for research and development but lack the large-scale manufacturing infrastructure needed to produce their creations in significant quantities.
