House Nomen (Military Household)

  • Level: 2
    • Level 1: 10 Force% worth of your army
      comes from this household, and behave
      at 1 Veterancy Level higher than what they should be.
    • Level 2: This 10 Force% also comes with a free Level 1 Fighter. Also receive a 5 Force% discount on a Level 2 military champion within your force who hails from this Household.
  • Champions: Skal
  • History: House Nomen is closely tied to the main family, Nomenica a connection reflected in their similar names. This household is responsible for producing elite bodyguards who serve individual members of the family, as well as fearsome melee soldiers renowned for their sheer brutality in combat.
