Overview #1

Negotiations Begin

Groups have begun dialogue with the Planetary Governors and those close to them in an attempt to determine what information can be gained. Meanwhile, other groups have begun investigating more covertly on the planet's surface to discover what is causing tithes not to be sent to Granvalis Prime.

The City

Ventoria City now has troops and aerospace units setting up around it, preparing for potential conflict. Meanwhile, covert operations proliferate within the city's bustling activity as forces dig in on the surface for possible future hostilities.
