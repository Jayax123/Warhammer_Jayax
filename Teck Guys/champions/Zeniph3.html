Guard Captain Veteran (Level 3)

  • Level 1 - 0% (Master-at-Arms)

    • This Captain can command units equivalent to a Company (10% force), unaffected by company doubling or supply purchases.

    • The unit this Captain leads acts 1 Veterancy higher than normal.

    • This Captain behaves as a Sub-OC.

  • Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • This captain can either halve allied casualties or double enemy casualties inflicted by their unit for 1 post per battle

    • The unit this Captain leads acts 2 Veterancy higher than normal.

  • Level 3 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • This captain can either halve allied casualties or double enemy casualties inflicted by their units for 2 posts per battle.

    • This captain can command 2 units at once.

    • The units this Captain leads acts 3 Veterancy higher than normal.

    • This Captain allows the force to do a 1 time deal: spend 10% to gain a full company of Imperial Guard.
