• Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • Unlocks more Custom Pattern modifications.

    • Can design Skitarii vehicles which the force has access to.

    • +10% to spend on Naval Modifications. Does not Stack with having additional Artisans.

    • This Artisan also comes with 2 Drop Building slots instead of 1.

    • Drop Buildings - 2 drop building slots, see the Support Regiments V1.0 - Imperial March “Drop Buildings” section.

  • Level 3 - 5% (Eugenics)

    • Allows for Custom Pattern modifications of Superheavy Vehicles.

    • All vehicles this Magos works on have +0.5 Internal Capacity.

    • This Artisan also comes with 3 Drop Building slots instead of 2.

    • +20% to spend on Naval Modifications. (On top of the Previous 10%) Does not Stack with having additional Artisans.

    • Up to 3 ships get 1 modification point by default of your choosing. Degradation Set to every 4 Modifications a ship has.

    • Can add Flare Shields to vehicles, but reduces their numbers by half.

    • Can place Drop Buildings on tracks for double price.
