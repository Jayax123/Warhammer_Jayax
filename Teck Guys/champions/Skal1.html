Skal

House: Nomen

Equipment: Conversion Field, Refractor Field, Dragonscale Armour, Power Sword, Volkite Serpenta, Digi Melta-Weapon

Background: Skal was first gifted to a powerful Inquisitor to work under them for House Nomen and thus Nomenica. Skal was later made a servitor by the Mechanicus when discovered but was recovered by House Nomen. Heavily augmented, Skal was given to Macille as a bodyguard, seen as having little value without his intelligence. With Macille         being a bastard son, it was thought fitting to         give Skal to him.
