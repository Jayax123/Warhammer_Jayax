• Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • A Lachrimallus can turn civilians and penals into Tech Thralls. They can do this at a rate of roughly 100 per day, and Tech Thralls are automatically equipped for their given purpose. They can also turn people into Servitors at a rate of 200 Servitor Points per day, if provided with equipment.

    • This Lachrimallus is able to create custom job servitors in campaigns if needed. If a servitor needs to be made which yells when it notices a xenos, or which needs to be able to calculate orbital trajectories, or any number of various tasks, a GM can give an arbitrary cost of making that servitor in Servitor Points and the Lachrimallus can make it quickly.
