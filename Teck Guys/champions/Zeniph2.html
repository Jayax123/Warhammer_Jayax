Expeditionary Captain (Inquisator): The commander for expeditionary forces for the Inquisitor, when troops need to be deployed.

  • Veterans: Specify 1 unit per 75 Force% as a “Veteran” unit. Give it full troop strength and gain +1 Veterancy for that unit.

  • Tactical and Strategic: Once per battle, may halve the casualties on Inquisitorial assets for a single post.

Type(s): Level 3 Militaris Savant, Level 3 Guard Captain Veteran

Militaris Savant (Level 3)

  • Level 1 - 10%

    • Grants an alternative ammo type for all connected solid shell forces (such as incendiary, AP, etc). This includes being able to provide Tank Companies with large amounts of AP shells, or Artillery companies with large numbers of Gas shells. They essentially act as an Imperial Guard Supply Distribution Company.

  • Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • Brings 2 units of Supplies for free per campaign.

    • Can set up train networks, utilizing 4 Rail-Layer vehicles which are received for free.

  • Level 3 - 5% (Eugenics)

    • Brings 3 units of Supplies for free per campaign (instead of 2)

    • Supplies have 4 companies worth of equipment instead of 3.

    • This Magos can secure 100 pieces of Rare Ammo instead of Supplies, such as Hunter Shells for Tanks and Photon Shells for Artillery, along with homebrewed shells (requires a Tech Priest Physic).

    • They can also obtain 2 companies worth of unmanned Guard vehicles instead, per maniple of supplies.
