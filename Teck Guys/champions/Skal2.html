Prime (Inquisator Rented): The champion of the Inquisitor, the individual the Inquisitor calls upon when an Astartes Champion is insulting their honor.

  • Butcher: This champion may “look out sir!” your commander when in close proximity.

Type(s): Level 3 Prime Savant, Level 3 Chirurgeon, Level 3 Fighter

Prime Savant (Level 3)

  • Level 1 - 10%

    • Starts with similar modifications and gear as a Heavy Skitarii, alongside a Conversion Field. Rare Weapon included.

    • Equivalent to a Vet 1 Astartes in terms of combat capabilities. Gains Veterancy similar to an Astartes.

  • Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • Grants the Tech Priest in question:

      • An extra foot of height (with increased strength and armor).

    • This Tech Priest is now roughly equivalent to a Vet 3 Astartes in terms of combat capabilities.

  • Level 3 - 5% (Eugenics)

    • Grants the Tech Priest in question:

      • An extra foot of height (with increased strength and armor).

    • This Tech Priest is now roughly equivalent to a Vet 5 Astartes in terms of combat capabilities, and gains the benefits of an Astartes Champion.
