House Faber

House Type: Level 2 Military Household

Champions: TTTTTT

House History: House Faber cares not for humanity; they care only for results and these results often involve the creation of grotesque amalgamations of flesh. House Faber frequently raids unfortified areas, kidnapping people and transforming them into servitor-like laborers or living weapons to be used against the House's enemies.

House Faber doesn't only do this to those they capture. The members of the House themselves often undergo inhuman modifications brutally efficient in both form and function. Some specialize in the creation of the House's constructs, reshaping their own bodies to incorporate circuitry and tools optimized for transforming others into amalgamations of flesh and metal.
