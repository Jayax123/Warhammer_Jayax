House Nomen

House Type: Military Household (No Levels Yet)

Champions: TTTTTT

House History: House Nomen, even by their name, is very closely tied to House Nomenica. They have served not only as loyal attendants but, more importantly, as bodyguards to House Nomenica for many years. They are raised with unwavering loyalty to the main house and are trained almost exclusively in the art of combat, capable of killing nearly anything. They are also enhanced with some of the finest augmentations and equipped with the best weapons available to excel in
their duties.
