House Hium

House Type: Advisor Household (No Level Currently)

House History: House Hium is a researcher of anything involving biology in service of Nomenica. It holds a seat of great power, with biology playing an essential part in many aspects of Nomenica and its subservient houses' endeavors, particularly in the realms of cloning and vitae womb use within the force. These are two major resources that almost every house uses in some capacity, and House Hium produces them for their use.
