House Rian

House Type: Level x Military Household

Champions: TTTTTT

House History: House Rian specializes in long-range weaponry, particularly advanced sniping technology—not only the weapons themselves, but also scopes and specialized equipment that snipers and spotters require to operate effectively. House Rian is also known for training some of the finest sharpshooters, capable of embedding themselves in hostile territory and eliminating high-value targets from extreme distances, often without ever being seen until it's far too late.

House Rian snipers usually operate in
pairs: a spotter and a sniper, both
augmented and trained very
differently. The spotter is
responsible for identifying and
tracking targets, providing
information to guide the sniper’s
shots. They also serve as a
bodyguard often augmented for
mid- to close-range combat and
equipped with weaponry suited
for such engagements. The
sniper, on the other hand, is
equipped almost exclusively for
long-range shooting. They are
typically fragile in close combat,
as their focus is entirely on
eliminating targets from a
distance. House Rian snipers are
considered among the best in their
field, highly specialized in their
roles.
