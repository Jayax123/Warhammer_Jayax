House Venus

House Type: Ship Household (No Level Currently)

Champions: TTTTTT

House History: House Venus specializes in space travel, designing ships and equipment specifically for operations in space. House Nomenica does not focus on space travel, relying instead on House Venus to supply and maintain its limited fleet. As the only household within House Nomenica that specializes in spacefaring technology, House Venus holds an irreplaceable position, ensuring that the greater house retains any capability in space operations.
