Skal

House: Nomen

Types: Level 2 Prime Savant (Vet 2),
Level 3 Chirurgeon, Level 3 Fighter

Prime Savant (Level 2)

  • Level 1 (10%)
    • Starts with similar modifications and gear as a Heavy Skitarii, alongside a Conversion Field. Rare Weapon included.
    • Equivalent to a Vet 1 Astartes in terms of combat capabilities. Gains Veterancy similar to an Astartes.
  • Level 2 (0% Eugenics)
    • Grants the Tech Priest in question: An extra foot of height (with increased strength and armor).
    • This Tech Priest is now roughly equivalent to a Vet 3 Astartes in terms of combat capabilities.

Fighter (Level 3)

  • Level 1 (0% Bodyguard)
    • This individual is an incredibly skilled fighter, a hair shy of an Astartes Neophyte. They usually have some degree of augmentation.
  • Level 2 (0% Eugenics)
    • This individual is usually given much heavier augmentation, to facilitate greater murder ability.
    • On par with an Astartes Neophyte.
  • Level 3 (0% Eugenics)
    • This individual is given the best augmentation possible from the character they serve.
    • On par with Astartes.
