Champion Levels

Level 3 Fabricator Savant

  • Level 1 - 10%

    • Starts with an Orbital Drop Factory, which can be dropped in from orbit onto the ground (See: Factory Lab).

    • Orbital Drop Factory: A deployable building which can be used to create materials for the war effort. They are usually defended with several heavy weapon emplacements. These can be used from orbit if desired, but will require a transport method for moving goods planetside. Costs 3 Maniples.

    • When one or multiple Factories or Strategic Engineer companies are working on projects in close proximity, this Tech Priest can monitor their progress. While monitoring, this Tech Priest can provide one of the following benefits. These benefits stack multiplicatively to a limit of 3, and can apply to up to 4 Factories or 4 Strategic Engineer Companies.

      • Production Speed 1.25x

      • Material Use 0.75x

    • +1 Influence to spend on Supplies in the Politics Lab.

  • Level 2 - 0% (Eugenics)

    • Production Speed benefit increased to 1.5x, and Material Use benefit increased to 0.5x.

    • New benefit below that can be chosen while overlooking production.

      • “Quality” Increased, a Guard level construction is further reinforced with Carapace Plating, or Carapace Plating is further reinforced with deep root structures. Equipment produced is first deemed to be Guard Level, then Storm Trooper Level.
